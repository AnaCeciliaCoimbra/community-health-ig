Related Person (TCCS)

This profile represents the constraints applied to the RelatedPerson resource by the Triage and Counseling Clinical System (TCCS) FHIR Implementation Guide. It describes the minimum expectations for the RelatedPerson resource when used in the system's composition or in one of the referred resources. The RelatedPerson resource is used to information about a person that is involved in the care for a patient, but who is not the target of healthcare, nor has a formal responsibility in the care process.
